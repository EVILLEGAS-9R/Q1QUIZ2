DESPICABLE ME

Despicable Me is a kids movie. It was made in 2010. It is a story about someone who traces the exploits of self-proclaimed villain Gru, whose life goal is to be internationally recognised as a master criminal. To achieve this goal, Gru has enlisted the help of sinister inventor Dr Nefario and hundreds of cute yellow creatures called 'minions'.
